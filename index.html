Publications & Preprints

RLCAD: Reinforcement Learning Training Gym for Revolution Involved CAD Command Sequence Generation

Under Review

Yin, X., Lu, X., Shen, J., Ni, J., Li, H., Tong, R., Tang, M., & Du, P.

arXiv preprint arXiv:2503.18549. Submitted to Computer-Aided Design.

arXiv

This paper presents RLCAD, a novel reinforcement learning framework for generating CAD command sequences from B-Rep geometries. Our method introduces a cross-modal policy network with dual attention mechanisms that learn to generate optimal command sequences by leveraging both geometric and topological features. Experiments demonstrate that RLCAD outperforms state-of-the-art methods in both command sequence generation accuracy and computational efficiency, achieving a 39X speedup over previous approaches. The framework supports a wider range of CAD operations, including revolution operations, making it suitable for more complex geometric modeling tasks.
